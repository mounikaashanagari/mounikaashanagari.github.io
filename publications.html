VISDB 2.0: A Curated Resource of Viral Integration Sites and Their Regulatory Landscapes in Human Diseases

Authors: C. Citu, A. Singh, X. Liu, M. Ashannagari, R. Ratnapriya, Z. Zhao

Publisher: Springer Nature

This publication presents VISDB 2.0, a curated resource of viral integration sites and associated regulatory landscapes in human diseases.

Research Contributions

  • Contributed to VISDB 2.0, a comprehensive database of virus-host integration sites with over 270,000 curated entries.
  • Supported the development of database pages for statistics, help documentation, and interactive visualization.
  • Created web-based visualizations for integration site distributions, host gene associations, and regulatory elements.
